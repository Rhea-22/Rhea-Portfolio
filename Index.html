About Art

I like to paint because it makes me calm. Whenever we feel angry or upset, we can learn to express our feelings on a piece of paper maybe. Sometimes we may feel bored and when that happens, painting is probably the best thing to do because it can help you to show how you feel. Even though paintings are usually complex and huge, I think that art is anything. Why I like to paint is so that I can develop different skill-sets and achieve more. That is why my favourite thing to do is paint. Not everyone's art can be perfect in one go because it takes practise to make perfect.
